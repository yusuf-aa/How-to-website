Here Are Some BasketBall Moves!!

Dribbling: Dribbling is when you bounce the ball with one hand while moving around the court. Dunking: Dunking is when you jump and forcefully put the ball through the hoop with one or both hands. Passing: Passing is when you throw the ball to a teammate to keep the play moving. Shooting: Shooting is when you aim and throw the ball to try and score in the basket. Blocking: Blocking is when you stop an opponentâ€™s shot by knocking the ball away as it goes up. Stealing: Stealing is when you take the ball from the other team without fouling. Pivoting: Pivoting is when you keep one foot still and turn your body to change direction. Layup: A layup is when you drive to the basket and softly shoot the ball off the backboard. Rebounding: Rebounding is when you grab the ball after someone misses a shot. Crossover: A crossover is when you quickly switch the ball from one hand to the other to fake out a defender. Pump Fake: A pump fake is when you pretend to shoot the ball to get the defender to jump early.
